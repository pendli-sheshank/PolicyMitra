## Section 2: Waiting Periods

<!-- clause: CL-WAIT-INITIAL-30D -->
There is an initial waiting period of 30 days from the date of policy commencement for all illnesses and their complications, except for hospitalization arising from an accident, which is covered from day one of the policy.

## Section 3: Room Rent & Accommodation

<!-- clause: CL-ROOM-RENT -->
The Room Category Limit under this Policy is capped at 1% of the Sum Insured per day for a Single Private Room. If the Insured Person occupies a room in excess of this Room Category Limit, all associated hospitalization expenses (including doctor's fees, nursing charges, and consumables) will be proportionately reduced in the same ratio as the actual room rent taken bears to the eligible Room Category Limit.

## Section 4: Co-payment

<!-- clause: CL-COPAY -->
A co-payment of 10% of the admissible claim amount is applicable to all claims where the Insured Person is aged 60 years or above at the time of hospitalization. No co-payment applies to Insured Persons below 60 years of age, except as specified for non-network hospitals under Section 6.

## Section 5: Exclusions

<!-- clause: CL-EXCL-COSMETIC -->
Cosmetic or plastic surgery, including any complications arising from such surgery, is excluded unless required to treat a covered accidental injury or as part of reconstructive surgery following a covered mastectomy or burn.

<!-- clause: CL-EXCL-DENTAL -->
Dental treatment or surgery of any kind, including implants, is excluded unless it requires hospitalization arising from an accident.

<!-- clause: CL-EXCL-INFERTILITY -->
Treatment relating to infertility, including assisted reproduction, In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF), and hormone therapy for the purpose of conception, is excluded.

## Section 6: Network Hospitals

<!-- clause: CL-NETWORK -->
The Policy provides cashless treatment access at 4,200 network hospitals across India. Treatment at a non-network hospital is reimbursed at the applicable rates, subject to an additional 10% co-payment on the admissible claim amount for such non-network treatment.

## Section 7: Portability

<!-- clause: CL-PORTABILITY -->
In accordance with IRDAI portability regulations, an Insured Person porting into this Policy from another health insurance policy shall be given credit for the time already served against the waiting periods for pre-existing diseases and specific illnesses, up to the waiting periods specified in Section 2 of this Policy, provided the porting application is made within the prescribed window before the expiry of the existing policy and there has been no break in coverage.
