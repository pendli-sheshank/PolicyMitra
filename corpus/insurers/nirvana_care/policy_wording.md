## Section 2: Waiting Periods

<!-- clause: CL-WAIT-INITIAL-15D -->
There is an initial waiting period of 15 days from the date of policy commencement for all illnesses and their complications, except for hospitalization arising from an accident, which is covered from day one of the policy.

## Section 3: Room Rent & Accommodation

<!-- clause: CL-ROOM-RENT -->
The Room Rent Sub-limit under this Policy is capped at 2% of the Sum Insured per day, subject to a maximum of ₹5,000 per day. If the Insured Person occupies a room in excess of this Room Rent Sub-limit, all associated hospitalization expenses (including doctor's fees, nursing charges, and consumables) will be proportionately reduced in the same ratio as the actual room rent taken bears to the eligible Room Rent Sub-limit.

## Section 4: Co-payment

<!-- clause: CL-COPAY -->
A flat co-payment of 10% of the admissible claim amount is applicable to all claims under this Policy, regardless of the Insured Person's age or the hospital's network status.

## Section 5: Exclusions

<!-- clause: CL-EXCL-COSMETIC -->
Cosmetic or plastic surgery, including any complications arising from such surgery, is excluded unless required to treat a covered accidental injury or as part of reconstructive surgery following a covered mastectomy or burn.

<!-- clause: CL-EXCL-DENTAL -->
Dental treatment or surgery of any kind, including implants, is excluded unless it requires hospitalization arising from an accident.

<!-- clause: CL-EXCL-MATERNITY -->
Maternity expenses, including childbirth and pre/post-natal care, are excluded unless the optional Maternity Care Rider has been purchased and its own 9-month waiting period has been served.

## Section 6: Network Hospitals

<!-- clause: CL-NETWORK -->
The Policy provides cashless treatment access at 3,400 network hospitals across India. Treatment at a non-network hospital is reimbursed at the applicable rates, subject to the flat 10% co-payment described in Section 4.

## Section 7: Portability

<!-- clause: CL-PORTABILITY -->
In accordance with IRDAI portability regulations, an Insured Person porting into this Policy from another health insurance policy shall be given credit for the time already served against the waiting periods for pre-existing diseases and specific illnesses, up to the waiting periods specified in Section 2 of this Policy, provided the porting application is made within the prescribed window before the expiry of the existing policy and there has been no break in coverage.
